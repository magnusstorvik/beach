Frei · Kristiansund · Norway

Vikaneset

Vikaneset lies by the sea on Frei, with accommodation, historic buildings and space for stays, gatherings and access to the water.

Several buildings of different age and use, gathered around a harbour on the south side of Kristiansund.

Accommodation

8 self catering units across two buildings

Sjøhus

5 units · Waterfront

Five self catering units on the waterfront. Each unit has a kitchen, bathroom and sleeping loft, with direct harbour access and its own balcony.

Sjøleiligheter

3 units

Three self catering units in the main building, each with its own kitchen and bathroom.

The site

A sequence of spaces across the property, from the main brygge hall to the harbour, shoreline and outdoor gathering area.

Klippfiskbrygga

Brygge building dating to approximately 1900 and renovated between 2006 and 2009. The main hall has exposed timber trusses, wooden floors and a stone fireplace, and is used for dining and events.

860 m² · Three storeys

Harbour and outbuildings

The property has harbour access with a pontoon, several brygge structures of different age and use, along with a naust and fjøs. Several structures are SEFRAK registered.

Amfi

Outdoor gathering space with stone terraced seating facing the water. Completed in 2008.

1,800 m² · Completed 2008

Shore and sauna

The site has sea access on three sides, with rocky shoreline and a sauna building as part of the wider setting.
